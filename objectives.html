Introduction to Entropy

  • Observe how particles and energy tend to spread throughout a system over time.
  • Describe entropy as a measure of the number of possible arrangements of a system.
  • Compare ordered and disordered states.
  • Explain why some processes occur naturally while their reverse processes are unlikely. 
  • Relate particle mixing to the concept of increasing memory. 
  • Understand why we cannot go back.
